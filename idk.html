Sweetie's Cafe <3

A unique and iconic place strongly linked to culture and luxury, Sweetie's Cafe embodies the soul of Paris. It is the perfect meeting of a Parisian where you can relax on the terrace while enjoying the view. Sweetie's cafe is one of the most beautiful secrets in Paris and also one of the best kept! Sweetie’s menu offers desserts cooked with love and quality products, which highlight seasonal fruits.
